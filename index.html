Tribute to Havildar Hangpan Dada

About Havildar Hangpan Dada

Havildar Hangpan Dada, born on 2nd October 1979, in Borduria village of Arunachal Pradesh, was a fearless soldier of the Indian Army who made the ultimate sacrifice for our nation. He displayed exceptional courage and valor in the line of duty.

Havildar Hangpan Dada served in the Assam Regiment and was a dedicated and patriotic soldier. He lost his life during a counter-terrorism operation in the Naugam sector of Jammu and Kashmir on May 26, 2016.

Havildar Hangpan Dada's unwavering commitment to the defense of our country and his supreme sacrifice exemplify the spirit of the Indian Armed Forces. He was posthumously awarded the Ashoka Chakra, India's highest peacetime military decoration, for his exceptional bravery and sacrifice.

His legacy lives on as a source of inspiration to all Indians and a reminder of the sacrifices made by our brave soldiers to protect our nation's integrity and sovereignty.
